Hemisphere Classification via Image Analysis (MATLAB)

Developed a MATLAB-based image analysis tool to classify whether a photograph was taken in the Northern or Southern Hemisphere. The program evaluated seasons based on colors and the sun's cardinal direction to infer the hemisphere in which the photo was taken. Inspired by my favorite game, Geoguessr.

  • Implemented image-based analysis in MATLAB
  • Analyzed seasonal vegetation and environmental indicators
  • Modeled color-logic based season inference
  • Constructed decision framework for hemisphere classification
